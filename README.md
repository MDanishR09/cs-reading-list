# Foundations of Computer Science: A Chronological Reading List

A curated collection of the most influential research papers, essays, and masterworks that shaped the landscape of modern computer science—spanning from foundational logic and hardware architecture to distributed systems and state-of-the-art artificial intelligence.

---

### 1. Theoretical Foundations & Logic

    * [Read Paper](https://www.cs.virginia.edu/~robins/Turing_Paper_1936.pdf)
* **[1948] A Mathematical Theory of Communication** - Claude Shannon
    * [Read Paper](https://web.archive.org/web/20150204015826/http://cm.bell-labs.com/cm/ms/what/shannonday/shannon1948.pdf)
* **[1965] Solution of a Problem in Concurrent Programming Control** - Edsger Dijkstra
    * [Read Paper](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/365559.365617)
* **[1968] Go To Statement Considered Harmful** - Edsger Dijkstra

* **[1971] The Complexity of Theorem-Proving Procedures** - Stephen Cook
